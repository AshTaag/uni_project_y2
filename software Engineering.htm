Software Engineering

Information and Technology College's Software Engineering programme provides you with the academic understanding and practical skills required to succeed in this industry that is
characterised by constant change and rapid pace.

Why study Software:

• High demand for software engineers in the job market makes this a profitable and fulfilling career choice.

• Complex problem solving skills are required for software engineering because it entails developing software to address user needs.

• Possibility to work on interesting projects: As a software engineer, you will have the chance to work on a variety of projects,
from developing mobile applications to building the upcoming web standards.

• Flexibility in the workplace: Software engineering expertise is transferable to a wide range of fields, including finance, healthcare, and entertainment.

• Chance to make a difference: Software developers can significantly improve people's lives through the development of new technologies or the enhancement of already existing ones.

If this interest you then Computer Science course at Information and Technology College is for you!

The module contained in this course are:

• Interaction Design
• Web Application Penetration Testing
• Fuzzy Logic & Knowledge Based Systems
• Data Mining
• Big Data & Business Models
• Computer Ethics and Privacy
• Development Project
