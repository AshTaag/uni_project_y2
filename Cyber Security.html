Cyber Security

Information and Technology College's cyber security programme is designed to provide you with the know-how and abilities you need in a number
of crucial areas. The purpose of the course is to give students a thorough and in-depth grasp of security principles and as well as practical.

Why study Cyber Security:

• The demand for skilled computer science professionals is consistently high, with many lucrative job opportunities available.

• Job security: Because cybersecurity threats are becoming more sophisticated and numerous, there will always be a demand for professionals
who can assist firms in defending against them.

• Personal satisfaction: Working in cybersecurity may be personally fulfilling for many people, who also appreciate the difficulties and problem-solving components of the industry.

• Impact on society: Cybersecurity is essential for shielding citizens, businesses, and governments from online dangers. By helping to keep people and organisations secure online, experts
in the sector can have a beneficial impact on society.

• Continuous learning is necessary for experts in the field of computer science to stay up to date because the discipline is continually changing. A career in this could be
interesting and demanding.

If this interest you then Computer Science course at Information and Technology College is for you!

The module contained in this course are:

• C Programming
• Computer Law and Cyber Security Management
• Linux Security
• Cyber Threat Intelligence and Incident Responset
• Malware Analysis
• Computer Ethics and Privacy
• Development Project
