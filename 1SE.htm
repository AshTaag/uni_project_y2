Software Engineering

Qualification Requirment

A/AS-levels: ABB. Two AS-levels considered in place of one A-level.

GCSE: Grade B/5 in Maths.

Access to HE Diploma: Pass relevant diploma with a minimum of 45 credits at level 3, 30 of which must be at Distinction..

English Language: IELTS 6.0 or equivalent.

Other information

Course code: G310

Duration: 3years full-time, 4 years with placement

Fees: Home/ Students: £9,250. International: £14,000

Module Leader: Mrs Boateng
